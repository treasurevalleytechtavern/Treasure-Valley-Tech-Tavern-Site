How We Help

If someone hears your name tonight, will they be able to find you tomorrow?

That's really what this comes down to.

Most bands are doing the hard part already. Playing shows. Writing music. Showing up.

But online, things get messy fast.

Links break. Content sits in phones. Pages exist, but don't really lead anywhere.

So when someone tries to look you up after a show, they either don't find you... or they don't stick around.

That's the gap we help close.

We Start With Where You Already Are

Every band is in a different place.

Some have content but it's scattered. Some are active but hard to find. Some are just getting started.

So we start by taking a look at what you already have.

  • Where your content actually lives
  • Whether people can find you when they try
  • What it feels like landing on your pages for the first time

Then we clean up what's getting in the way and give you a clear direction forward.

No pressure. Just a starting point.

Let's Get You Set Up

Not every band needs the same level of help.

Some just need things cleaned up. Some need help staying active. Some need someone there capturing what's happening. Some want to hand it off completely.

Here's how that usually looks.

Let Us Help You

Load-In

Get everything set up so people can actually find you.

We take a look at what you already have and clean it up so it works the way it should.

Includes
  • Review of your pages, including Instagram, TikTok, website, and more
  • Fixing broken or missing links
  • Bio and profile clarity
  • Basic organization, like a link hub or pinned content
  • Clear recommendations on what to do next
Best For

Bands that feel scattered, hard to find, or not sure what's working.

Fix My Setup

Backline

Keep your socials running without thinking about it.

You already have content. We make sure it actually gets posted and seen.

Includes
  • Posting 2 to 4 times per week
  • Captions, tagging, and formatting
  • Scheduling across platforms
  • Light repurposing of existing content
  • Story posts around shows and announcements
Best For

Bands who have content but don't post consistently.

Keep Me Posting

Camera Op

Capture what's actually happening.

We show up and film your band in real moments so you actually have content to use.

Includes
  • Live show footage
  • Practice and behind-the-scenes clips
  • Short-form ready video clips
  • Organized delivery so you can post it yourself
Best For

Bands who don't have enough content or don't want to deal with filming.

Come Film Us

Road Crew

We're in it with you. Capturing, posting, and keeping you visible.

We handle both sides. Creating content and keeping your pages active.

Includes
  • Everything from Camera Op and Backline
  • Content captured at shows or practice
  • Turned into posts and scheduled consistently
  • Posted across your platforms
  • Featured on our page to help promote you
Best For

Bands who want to focus on playing and not deal with the internet.

Handle Everything

How We Approach This

We're not here to turn your band into a production set.

We use lightweight gear so we can actually move with the moment and not slow it down.

Live shows. Practice sessions. The in-between moments that make your band feel like you.

The goal is not to make you look different. It's to make sure people actually see what's already there.

We're Part Of This Scene Too

This started as a passion project and it still feels like one. But we take showing up for people seriously.

We're not trying to over-polish anything. We're not trying to turn local music into a content machine.

We just want:

  • Bands to be easier to find
  • Shows to feel alive online
  • The local scene to actually connect

You make the noise. We'll handle the internet.

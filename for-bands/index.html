You make the noise, we'll handle the internet.

IF SOMEONE HEARS YOUR NAME TONIGHT, WILL THEY BE ABLE TO FIND YOU TOMORROW?

That's really what this comes down to.

Most bands are doing the hard part already. Playing shows. Writing music. Showing up.

But online, things get messy fast.

Links break. Content sits in phones. Pages exist, but don't really lead anywhere.

So when someone tries to look you up after a show, they either don't find you... or they don't stick around.

That's the gap we help close.

WE START WITH WHERE YOU ALREADY ARE

Every band is in a different place.

Some have content but it's scattered. Some are active but hard to find. Some are just getting started.

So we start by taking a look at what you already have.

  • Where your content actually lives
  • Whether people can find you when they try
  • What it feels like landing on your pages for the first time

Then we clean up what's getting in the way and give you a clear direction forward.

No pressure. Just a starting point.

LET'S GET YOU SET UP

Not every band needs the same level of help.

Some just need things cleaned up. Some need help staying active. Some need someone there capturing what's happening. Some want to hand it off completely.

Here's how that usually looks.

HOW WE APPROACH THIS

We're not here to turn your band into a production set.

We use lightweight gear so we can actually move with the moment and not slow it down.

Live shows. Practice sessions. The in-between moments that make your band feel like you.

The goal is not to make you look different. It's to make sure people actually see what's already there.

Treasure Valley Tech Tavern is a Boise-based digital stagehand supporting bands across the Treasure Valley and the western United States. A digital stagehand is exactly what it sounds like, someone behind the scenes making sure everything runs smoothly, just online instead of on stage. We handle the heavy lifting that keeps your digital presence in place, updating tour dates, linking tickets, organizing your music and media, and keeping your website and socials consistent so nothing falls through the cracks.

It's the same role as in a venue, moving things into place, keeping everything connected, and making sure the show runs smoothly. The difference is we're doing it for your online presence, where most fans decide whether or not they're coming to your show. From local Boise bands playing their first sets to touring artists passing through Idaho, we build and maintain simple, reliable digital homes for music. No overcomplicated systems, no corporate marketing layers, just practical support that works for real bands. If your music is out in the world but your online presence feels scattered, outdated, or hard to navigate, that's where we come in.

Based in Boise, Idaho. Supporting the Treasure Valley music scene and touring bands across the West.

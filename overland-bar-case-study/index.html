WEBSITE

We built and manage Overland Bar's website end-to-end, keeping it simple, current, and reflective of what's actually happening in the bar.

The main page gives a clear overview of what Overland offers, while individual sections are designed to be genuinely useful. The karaoke page makes it easy for people to tip KJs, and the menu stays updated as drinks change.

The goal is not just to have a website, but to have one that people can actually use and understand without digging.

TIKTOK

We created and manage Overland Bar's TikTok, posting daily clips that capture real moments from the bar.

Starting from zero, the account has grown to over 1,100 followers and 200K+ views in the last 60 days, with most views coming through the For You page and reaching a strong local audience.

People are engaging with the content, recognizing the bar, and even mentioning it directly to the owner.

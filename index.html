Making the Treasure Valley scene easier to find, follow, and show up for

Most people in the Treasure Valley want to support local bands and venues.

They just do not know where to start.

They will hear a band name and try to look them up and find nothing clear.

They will look for something to do on a weekend and miss shows that are already happening.

Most of what people see are the big touring acts coming through, while the local scene, the regular nights, and the smaller shows stay harder to find.

Not because the scene is not active, but because it is hard to find unless you are already part of it.

That is the gap we focus on.

Treasure Valley Tech Tavern acts as a digital stagehand for the scene, making sure what is happening in real life actually shows up online.

Not everything here has to apply to you. We build around what you actually need and ignore what you do not.

For Bands

What Is Getting In The Way

  • People hear your name but cannot find you
  • Your shows are not listed in one clear place
  • Your socials do not reflect how active you actually are
  • You have content, but it is scattered, unposted, or lost
  • Every platform works differently, and it is hard to keep up

How We Help

We help you get organized, visible, and easy to follow without having to think about every platform.

  • Set up a clean home base, like a site or link hub
  • Make sure your shows are easy to find
  • Align your socials so everything points to the right place
  • Capture real moments from live shows, practice, and the in-between
  • Shape and post that content across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook in a way that fits each platform

We handle how things show up online so you can focus on making music and playing shows.

For Bars & Venues

What Is Getting In The Way

  • What is happening this week is not obvious unless someone already knows
  • Your schedule is spread across posts, stories, or not clearly listed at all
  • Your socials feel inconsistent or quiet
  • New customers do not understand your vibe before they walk in
  • Posting regularly across platforms takes time you do not have

How We Help

We help you stay clear, active, and easy to walk into without needing to manage it day to day.

  • Clean up and align your online presence
  • Make your weekly schedule clear and easy to find
  • Set up a simple, mobile-friendly site or page
  • Optimize your Google listing so people can actually find you
  • Capture the energy of your space and turn it into content
  • Post and schedule that content across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube so your pages stay active

We handle the platforms and the posting so your space stays visible without constant effort.

MAKING THE TREASURE VALLEY SCENE EASIER TO FIND, FOLLOW, AND SHOW UP FOR

Most people in the Treasure Valley want to support local bands and venues.

They just don’t know where to start.

They hear a band name and try to look them up. Nothing clear.
They look for something to do on a weekend and miss shows that are already happening.

Not because the scene isn’t active.
Because it’s hard to find unless you’re already part of it.

That’s the gap we focus on.

Treasure Valley Tech Tavern acts as a digital stagehand for the scene.
Making sure what’s happening in real life actually shows up online.

So when someone is looking for something to do, they land on you.

Not everything here has to apply to you.
We build around what you actually need and ignore what you don’t.

FOR BANDS

WHAT IS GETTING IN THE WAY

  • People hear your name... and can't find you
  • Your shows aren't listed in one clear place
  • Your socials don't reflect how active you actually are
  • You have content... but it is scattered, unposted, or lost
  • Every platform works differently. It's hard to keep up

HOW WE HELP

We help you get organized, visible, and easy to follow without having to think about every platform

  • Set up a clean home base, like a site or link hub
  • Make sure your shows are easy to find
  • Align your socials so everything points to the same place
  • Capture real moments from live shows, practice, and the in-between
  • Shape and post that content across platforms in a way that fits each one

We handle how things show up online so you can focus on making music and playing shows.

FOR BARS & VENUES

WHAT IS GETTING IN THE WAY

  • What is happening this week isn't obvious unless someone already knows
  • Your schedule is spread across posts, stories, or not clearly listed at all
  • Your socials feel inconsistent... or just quiet
  • New customers don't understand your vibe before they walk in
  • Posting regularly across platforms takes time you don't have

HOW WE HELP

We help you stay clear, active, and easy to walk into without needing to manage it day to day.

  • Clean up and align your online presence
  • Make your weekly schedule clear and easy to find
  • Set up a simple, mobile-friendly site or page
  • Optimize your Google listing so people can actually find you
  • Capture the energy of your space and turn it into content
  • Post and schedule that content across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube so your pages stay active

We handle the platforms and the posting so your space stays visible without constant effort.

Built for bars & venues

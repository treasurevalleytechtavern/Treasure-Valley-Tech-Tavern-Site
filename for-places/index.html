For Bars & Venues

If someone is deciding where to go tonight, will they find you?

That's really what this comes down to.

Most bars and venues are already doing the hard part.

Running nights. Hosting events. Building a real crowd.

But online, it falls apart fast.

Whether you're a neighborhood bar, a dedicated venue, or something in between.

Schedules are buried in posts. Stories disappear. Pages exist, but do not clearly say what's happening.

So when someone's looking for something to do, they either do not find you, or they do not understand why they should go.

That's the gap we help close.

We make it obvious what you are, what's happening, and why someone should walk through your door tonight.

We Start With Where You Already Are

Every bar and venue is different.

Some have a strong regular crowd but do not show up online. Some post, but it is inconsistent. Some are active, but it is hard to tell what's happening on any given night.

So we do not start from scratch.

We start by looking at what already exists:

  • Where your information actually lives
  • Whether people can find you when they try
  • What it feels like landing on your pages for the first time

Then we clean up what's getting in the way and give you a clear direction forward.

No overhaul. No pressure.

Just making what you already have actually work.

Let's Get You Set Up

Not every venue needs the same level of help.

Some just need things cleaned up.

Some need someone capturing what's actually happening in the room.

Some want it handled so they do not have to think about it at all.

Here's how that usually looks.

Setup

Make it easy for people to find you and immediately understand what's happening.

We take a look at what you already have and clean it up so it works the way it should, so someone new can understand your space without guessing.

Includes
  • Review of your pages, including Instagram, Facebook, Google, website, and more
  • Fixing incorrect hours, links, or missing information
  • Clear weekly schedule setup so people know what's happening each night
  • Bio and profile clarity, including what you are, what you do, and what nights matter
  • Basic organization like a link hub or simple mobile-friendly page
  • Google Business cleanup and optimization
  • Up to 10 QR table or bar inserts, sized 3x5 or 4x6
  • Clear recommendations on what to do next
Best For

Bars and venues that feel scattered, are hard to find, or where people do not know what's happening.

Fix My Setup

In The Room

Show people what it actually feels like to be in your room.

We show up and capture real moments as they're happening, and give you content that shows your bar or venue the way it actually is.

Includes
  • On-site content capture through short visits, not full-night coverage
  • Footage of crowd, vibe, bartenders, events, and atmosphere
  • Short-form video clips ready for Instagram, TikTok, and more
  • Light editing to keep things clean and usable
  • Organized delivery so you can post it yourself
  • Content that reflects real energy, not staged or overproduced
Best For

Bars and venues that want content but do not have time, or someone, to capture it.

Come Capture Our Space

All Hands

We're in it with you, capturing, posting, and keeping you visible.

We handle both sides: creating content and making sure it actually gets out into the world consistently.

Includes
  • Everything from Setup and In The Room
  • Content captured at your space on a consistent schedule
  • Turned into short-form posts and scheduled out
  • Posting across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and more
  • Captions, tagging, and formatting handled
  • Ongoing schedule updates and visibility support
  • Featured on our page when applicable to help promote your space
Best For

Bars and venues that want to stay active online without having to manage it day to day.
